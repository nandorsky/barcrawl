A tradition unlike any other.
A trip back through time to a simpler place.

It is with great pleasure and overly intense anticipation that we announce the 3rd Annual Post-Grad Time Machine Bar Crawl will be Saturday, May 10th. For those who have partaken in this adventure before, welcome back. For those joining us for the first time, may God have mercy on your soul.

The Post-Grad Time Machine Bar Crawl began as a way for a few George Washington University alums to relive college days in their old college bars. What may have started as a small blackout gathering has grown to hundreds of degenerates traveling across our nation's capitol on a armada of school buses. Welcome participants not only include GWU alums from DC and around the country, but more importantly, we've been joined by our friends, family, coworkers and random acquaintances outside of the GW family.

schedule
As we did last year, we will go to a collection of bars that we crushed in the mid-late 2000s in relatively chronological order. We will begin at the Exchange in the early afternoon and a bus will take us from there to each and every stop along this epic journey.
