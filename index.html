A tradition unlike any other.
A trip back through time to a simpler place.

It is with great pleasure and overly intense anticipation that I announce the 3rd Annual Post-Grad Time Machine Bar Crawl will be Saturday, May 10th. For those who have partaken in this adventure, welcome back. For those joining us for the first time, may God have mercy on you soul.

The Post-Grad Time Machine Bar Crawl began as a way for a few degenerate George Washington University alums to relive college days in their old college bars. What started as a small blackout gathering has grown to a 150 person bus armada of not only GWU alums, but more importantly, our friends, family, coworkers and random acquaintances. As we did last year, we will go to a collection of bars that were crushed in the mid-late 2000s in a relatively chronological order.
